Кто же такой этот Чигири Хёма?

Chigiri from a very young age has always thought himself superior to others due to his naturally strong muscles, amazing skill in football, and his outstanding speed. He for awhile looked down on those who got in his way or annoyed him but was mostly modest. Only when he tears his ACL does his sense of superiority start to crumble and due to being harassed by the Wanima Brothers he becomes afraid of playing football wholeheartedly. He becomes so afraid of never playing football again that he rather find a reason to quit than to persevere for his dream.

When retaking a hold of his ego and ambition, Chigiri has shown to be a persistent and stubborn force on the field. He always strives to leave an effect on the field, which causes other players to notice him and acknowledge his abilities. Chigiri is shown to have great focus being a player who is constantly running through tight spaces, between players, and even switching from dribbling to passing. Chigiri admits that he feels a great joy surpassing other players with his speed, and when is inspired by Isagi and retakes his will to play and win he finally leaves behind his fear and begins to once again be that player he used to be in middle school and states he will become the best striker in the world.

He enjoys taking care of his appearance often like blow drying and taking care of his hair and many other player s have noticed. He also speaks about how he cares towards his injured knee.

Chigiri is also easy-going and friendly outside of the field. While on the Team Z, he often talks casually with Kunigami and eventually makes a team with him fully trusting in each other, demonstrating a great chemistry on the field. After he joins Team White in the Second Selection, he is seen to get along very well with Nagi, watching together videos about professional players and latter playing together in a contest with shoes in which they compite to see which one of them can dominate with their shoes making a mess in their bedroom.
