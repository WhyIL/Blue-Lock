Next character is a Anri Teieri

Anri Teieri (帝襟 アンリ Teieri Anri?) is a new hire of the Japan Football Union. Sometime after the defeat of the Japan National Team in the 2018 World Cup, Anri proposes to the executives of the Japan Football Union a plan to help Japan succeed in winning the World Cup.

Anri is a calm but very determined young woman whose biggest dream is to see Japan win the World Cup. Anri is passionate when it comes to football and although the majority in the Japan Football Association ridiculed her and treat her as nothing more than a woman with big dreams and a big chest, she sticks to her goals and visions. She also speaks her mind, unaffected by the business-minded Japan Football Association ideologies.
